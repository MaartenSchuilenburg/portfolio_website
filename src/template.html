Pharmalarm

In October 2019 the 'Tweede Kamer' held a hackathon with a goal to improve the negotiating position of the Netherlands with Pharmaceutical companies. With the help of open data, we combined the medicine prices of six other countries and created an easy to use web application which parliament members can use to filter different medicines and vendors. This could then be used to decrease the total amount the Dutch government pays for medicine by as much as 150 million euros.
